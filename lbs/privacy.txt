Privacy policy for lend ad borrow recorder application

This privacy policy covers following 

We do not use users data for any purpose and application is full feature oriented to use local storage
for any records of data.  User can feel free and safe to use this app. Files access permission is requested 
only to backup and restore the recorded data.

Permission requested
android.permission.RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED. Needed to notify the app after reboot
android.permission.WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE. Needed to backup data 
android.permission.READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE.  Needed to restore data
